Who I Am & What I Do

I am ZAHOOR AHMAD, a PhD researcher working in the field of Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), specializing in biofluid flow modeling using Artificial Neural Networks and Physics-Informed Neural Networks. My research focuses on flow behavior in non-uniform channels, where I integrate numerical simulations and machine learning techniques to analyze velocity, pressure, and shear stress characteristics of biofluids such as blood. In addition to my research, I have 5 years of experience in university teaching for different subjects as a visiting lecturer, mentoring students and guiding projects in simulation and modeling.
